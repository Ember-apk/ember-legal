Privacy Policy

Ember is currently in beta. During this period, service availability may be limited, features may change without notice, and registration may be temporarily restricted. This Privacy Policy applies to all versions of the app including beta releases.

This Privacy Policy explains how Ember Project ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Ember mobile application ("the App").

Ember is designed with privacy as its core principle. We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service and we are technically unable to read your messages.

1. Who We Are

Operator: Ember Project

Country: Romania, European Union

Contact: emberproject.app@gmail.com

This application is intended for use by residents of the European Economic Area (EEA) only.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data stored on our server

  • Username — encrypted with AES-256-GCM. We store an encrypted version of your username. We are technically unable to read it without the encryption key, and we do not associate it with any real-world identity.
  • Password — stored as a bcrypt hash (cost 12). We cannot recover it.
  • Safety key hash — SHA-256 hash. We cannot recover the original key.
  • Public encryption key — visible by design, used to encrypt messages sent to you.
  • Push notification tokens (Expo and FCM) — used to deliver messages to your device.
  • Device ID — a hash generated on your device, used to link your account to your specific device.
  • Registration timestamp — when your account was created.
  • Last activity timestamp — updated when you log in. Used for inactivity-based account deletion.

2.2 Data in server logs

Our server (Hetzner, Helsinki, Finland) temporarily logs:

  • IP address — recorded for authentication endpoints only (/auth/login, /auth/register, /auth/refresh).
  • Timestamp — of each request.
  • Endpoint accessed — which API endpoint was called.

✓ Message endpoints (/message/) are explicitly excluded from logging. We do not log who you message or who messages you.

Logs are automatically deleted after 7 days.

2.3 Data processed by third parties

  • Expo Push Service (expo.dev) — receives your push token and an encrypted, opaque payload. Cannot read message content.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — delivers push notifications on Android. Receives push token and encrypted payload only.
  • Hetzner Cloud — our server infrastructure provider in Helsinki, Finland (EU).
  • Google Play Store (if downloaded via Play Store) — Google may associate your download with your Google account, according to Google's own privacy policy.
  • GitHub (if downloaded via direct APK) — GitHub/Microsoft logs your IP address at the time of download. We recommend using a VPN to protect your IP address during download.

2.4 Data stored locally on your device

  • Your private encryption key — never transmitted to our server.
  • Your username, session tokens, device ID.
  • Pending messages — stored temporarily until read, then deleted.
  • Contacts — stored locally only, never sent to the server.
  • Achievements (ash count, streak, ghost count) — stored locally only, never sent to the server.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • ✓ Message content — end-to-end encrypted, technically impossible for us to read.
  • ✓ Sender of messages — Sealed Sender protocol hides the sender from our server.
  • ✓ Recipient of messages — suppressed from server logs.
  • ✓ Your real identity — no name, email address, or phone number required.
  • ✓ Your location — never collected.
  • ✓ Your contacts — stored locally on your device only.
  • ✓ Message history — messages are ephemeral and deleted after being read or after the timer expires.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — to provide the Ember service to you.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — to maintain server security and prevent abuse.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR) — to comply with applicable law when required.

5. Data Retention

  • Server logs — deleted automatically after 7 days.
  • Account data — retained while your account is active.
  • Inactive accounts — you receive a push notification after 23 days of inactivity. Your account is automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Deleted accounts — all data removed immediately. Username reserved permanently to prevent reuse.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a resident of the EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request information about data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — delete your account at any time within the app, or by contacting us.
  • Right to portability — request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to restriction — request restriction of processing.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with your national data protection authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: emberproject.app@gmail.com

Romanian supervisory authority: ANSPDCP (www.dataprotection.ro)

7. Data Transfers

Your data is stored on servers in Helsinki, Finland (EU). Push notification delivery involves Expo (USA) and Google FCM (USA). Both are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses under GDPR.

8. Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • TLS 1.3 for all communications
  • End-to-end encryption (Curve25519 + XSalsa20-Poly1305)
  • Sealed Sender protocol
  • bcrypt password hashing (cost factor 12)
  • AES-256-GCM username encryption
  • Rate limiting on all endpoints
  • Account locked to a single device
  • Screenshot protection enabled globally
